### Abstract Submission

**Abstract submission is open.**

SACB 2027 is accepting abstract submissions (max 500 words) for oral and poster presentations from students, postdocs, and faculty across six sessions: Tumor Heterogeneity & Metastasis, Metabolism Across Scales, Immunology & Host-Tumor Interactions, Systems Pharmacology & Translational Systems Biology, Targeting Signal Transduction, and Spatial Single-Cell Cancer Systems Biology, with two oral slots per session plus interactive poster sessions. The meeting will also feature a Postdoc Highlights session, where the organizing committee selects 4–5 standout abstracts from postdoc presenters to showcase especially innovative and impactful work, offering strong visibility within the cancer systems biology community. **The application deadline is Friday, October 16, 2026, with notifications sent in November.**

Please submit you Abstract [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeBwJBM0JkujCIcKF_ZFC_2Ie3tMv50Zkit6DfR0sWv6Gahgg/viewform?usp=publish-editor).

### SACB Scholars Submission

SACB Scholar submission is open

We are accepting applications for the 2027 SACB Scholars Program, which offers travel fellowships covering travel, lodging, food, and conference costs, along with a peer mentor, to boost early-career trainee participation in cancer systems biology. Undergraduates, graduate students, and postdocs in STEM or medical fields, including adjacent quantitative disciplines—are encouraged to apply, with no prior cancer systems biology experience required. Instead of an abstract, applicants submit a brief essay on their interest in the field, and priority is given to U.S. applicants due to limited funding. **The application deadline is Friday, October 16, 2026, with notifications sent by late November.**

Please submit your Application [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfBt9x7KAg_7UO7WINYVOuCtopqNQKg6Mi6FOPZErhIKajbLg/viewform?usp=dialog).
